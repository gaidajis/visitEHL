Pully Beach

Urban beach and swimming complex 10 minutes east of Ouchy. Features Olympic-size pools, direct lake access, beach volleyball, and a lawn with alpine views. Perfect for a Wednesday afternoon dip.

Flon District

Former 19th-century warehouse quarter transformed into Lausanne's creative hub. Bowling at Bowl Bar, pop-up urban beach (summer), ice rink (winter), avant-garde theatre at Arsenic, and densest concentration of bars/clubs.

Bowling Arsenic Theatre Nightlife Boutiques

Ouchy Lakefront

Belle Époque promenade with the Olympic Museum gardens, the iconic Olympic flame monument, and views across Lake Geneva to the French Alps. Free stroll, perfect for Monday evening.

Olympic Museum Promenade Gardens

Cully Village

Quaint Lavaux wine village directly on the lake. Walkable from the train station. Stone houses, waterfront restaurants, and immediate access to the UNESCO vineyard hiking trails. Less touristy than Lutry.

12 min from Lausanne Wine Bars Village Charm
Campus Practical Info

Campus is cashless — all credit cards including Amex accepted. Reception: Mon–Fri 08:00–19:00, Sat 08:00–18:00, Sun Closed. Breakfast, lunch & dinner normally served on campus Mon–Fri.

Campus Overview

Self-contained hospitality village on the heights of Lausanne. Modern architecture set in green parkland with views toward the lake. Business guests have access to world-class student-operated facilities.

On-Campus Dining

Berceau des Sens (Michelin-starred, student-run) and 1893 (French brasserie) serve Mon–Fri. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner are normally available for campus guests. Sunday = closed.
